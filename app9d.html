Master Spatial & Conceptual Frameworks

In university entry assessments, prepositions form the core structural glue. Explore key frameworks below, then move to the Practice Arena to reinforce your memory tracks.

🕒

Prepositions of Time

  • at → Exact points (at 7:00 AM, at noon)
  • on → Days / Dates (on Monday, on May 15th)
  • in → Broad frames (in the morning, in 2026)
📍

Spatial Frameworks

  • at → Specific targets (at the library entrance)
  • on → Surfaces / Lines (on textbook page 45)
  • in → Enclosed volumes (in the science lab)
📝

Academic Collocations

  • depend on → Determined by (Grades depend on scores.)
  • participate in → Take part (Participate in clubs.)
  • focus on → Concentrate (Focus on study tasks.)
